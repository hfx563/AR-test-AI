-------------------------

4. Open: READY_TO_UPLOAD\index.html
5. Copy all content (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C)
6. Paste in GitHub (Ctrl+V)
7. Scroll down, add message: "Updated index.html"
8. Click "Commit changes"

Repeat for app.js and style.css
