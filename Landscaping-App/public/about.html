About GreenEdge

Family-owned landscaping resources and supply company serving the region since 2009.

Our Story

Built on hard work, trust, and a love for the outdoors.

GreenEdge was founded in 2009 with a single truck and a commitment to quality supply. Today, we operate a full fleet delivering bulk materials, plants, and equipment to landscaping contractors across the region.

We invest in technology — including our NFC-based truck tracking system — to ensure every delivery is accountable, efficient, and on time.
